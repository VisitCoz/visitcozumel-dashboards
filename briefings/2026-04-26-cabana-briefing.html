Context · why we're doing this

The opportunity, in plain words.

Celebrity has dozens of Cozumel excursions, but no fully private cabana on its shelf. Their existing beach products — Kuza VIP, La Casa, Barracuda — are all shared beach clubs at $137–209 per guest. We have access to Cabana Beach (Tan Navarro's venue at the south end of Chankanaab Park). Tan does not market to cruise lines directly. That's our wedge.

Why now

Mike pitched this at Seatrade Miami on April 15 — Matthew and Adam were receptive. Native Kitchen took ~3 weeks from pitch to approval. We want to move before momentum fades.

Why two products

Beach Club competes with La Casa and Barracuda on price. Private Cabana owns the white space — nothing else on Celebrity's Cozumel shelf is fully private. Two SKUs gives Celebrity flexibility to upsell across guest types.

Why per-pax for one, per-cabana for the other

Beach Club is shared — strangers fill the van together — so per-pax pricing is natural. Private Cabana is exclusive — one party per cabana — so flat per-cabana pricing protects the privacy promise. Couple pays the same as a family of six.

Why Cabana Beach

Tan offers wholesale rates: cabana 50% off, food package 25% off, park entry direct to Mike at $20/pax (vs $29 retail). Twelve cabanas — small enough to feel exclusive, big enough to scale. On-site chef, beach access, snorkel, all built in.

Product A · Catalog listing

Cozumel Hideaway: Beach Club & Caribbean Feast

An exclusive small beach club at Chankanaab Park, with chef-prepared lunch, open bar, and reef snorkeling included.

Overview

Leave behind the worries of everyday life for a tropical getaway and enjoy the amenities of Cozumel's most exclusive small beach club, including direct reef snorkeling, a chef-prepared lunch, and an open bar.

Highlights

Board your air-conditioned van and enjoy a 15-minute scenic drive to Cabana Beach, a small private retreat tucked into the south end of Chankanaab Park. This is a secluded venue that operates only when a small group is booked — never crowded, never shared with another tour.

Soak in the tropical sun by the Caribbean's pristine waters, sip a refreshing cocktail from the bartender, and drift to the soothing sounds of the waves. Snorkel directly off the legendary Chankanaab Reef, just steps from your lounger — no boat, no waiting.

Prepare to be pampered — the venue comes with a gracious bilingual host, attentive waiters, and an on-site chef who prepares a delicious plated lunch: two house dishes and a dessert, paired with an open bar of soft drinks, ice-cold beer, and domestic spirits.

Feeling adventurous? Explore Chankanaab's full grounds — the natural lagoon, gardens, and trails are all yours, since park entry is included. Or receive a relaxing massage from on-site experts (available at an additional cost) — the magic of Cozumel is yours to discover.

After unforgettable memories, return to your awaiting ship feeling fully refreshed.

Important details & advisements

  • This is an exclusive small beach club — the tour operates only when a minimum of 8 guests is booked.
  • If guests would like to snorkel, they need to know how to swim and be physically fit enough to snorkel by themselves. Pregnant guests and those with asthma, epilepsy, diabetes, or recent surgeries are not allowed to snorkel.
  • Only foldable wheelchairs can be accommodated on this tour.
  • Guests must sign a waiver to participate.
