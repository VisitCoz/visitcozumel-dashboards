Dear Mariel,

Thank you for the invitation to participate in the TUI Cruises 2026–29 RFP, and our apologies for replying right at the deadline rather than earlier in the window.

I want to be transparent about one constraint: Cozumel's transportation availability is currently very tight. For the contract dates we were unable to secure additional vans, and we were only able to commit one additional coach beyond our existing Cozumel Highlights operation with Intercruises. With that constraint in mind, we focused on a single new tour designed to maximize ship-call utility — rather than spreading our limited fleet across multiple tours that would underperform.

Our proposal for the 2026–29 program is:

Das Geheimnis der Maya · Sterne, Sonne, Tempel
The Secret of the Maya: Stars, Sun, Temples

A 2-hour cultural excursion with two stops:

  1. Plaza de las Dos Culturas — a guided cultural walk through the historic Cozumel plaza where Mayan and Spanish-Mexican cultures met, including the story of Ixchel and how the Maya worshipped their gods and temples.
  2. The 360° immersive fulldome cinema — a 20-minute Mayan-archaeoastronomy film, followed by a 25-minute interactive live segment with our astronomy guide. Guests learn about the Maya, the temples, and the stars — and volunteer guests' birthdays are translated into the Mayan Long Count and projected on the dome.

Because the tour runs only 2 hours, our single dedicated coach can complete 4 rotating departures per ship call from 08:00 onwards — serving up to 180 passengers per ship call at €45 retail per adult. We believe this is the most TUI-guest-friendly use of the transportation we were able to secure for the contract.

The full SED is in the NEW1 / VISIT tab of the attached workbook. The TO Information and Guide & Port Information tabs are also completed. The Private Arrangements tab is left blank — we did not have time to compile a competitive pricing schedule for that section, and would prefer to discuss separately if TUI is interested.

I will follow up tomorrow with:

  • Our current civil-liability + tour-operator insurance certificate (PDF)
  • A complete overview of the ferry fleet + CZM–PCM transfer costs

Thank you again for your patience, and please let us know if you need anything adjusted or expanded before TUI's review.
