internal note · where our product sits on Celebrity's shelf

The shelf we are pricing into.

The clearest comparable in the market right now is Carnival's Mayan Interactive Park, Planetarium & Lunch at $79.99 retail. We do not want to price at Carnival's retail — we want to price into Celebrity's premium "Small Group" shelf, which sits above it.

The two shelves

Mass-market · the floor

The math: at a typical 40–50% cruise-line markup, Carnival is paying their operator roughly $40–$48 net per pax. That is the cost-plus mass-market shelf. We are not competing on this shelf.
Premium small-group · our shelf
Celebrity · Small Group
Beyond the Mayan Sky · A Watercolor Workshop with a Live Astronomer
3 hr max 24 pax private van named artist take-home painting live astronomer
$85 / pax net VAT-incl · recommended open
The math: Celebrity retails this at their standard 40–50% markup → $119–$128 retail per guest. That sits cleanly in the cultural-shorex band ($89–$179) and well below the premium ceiling. Different shelf from Carnival entirely.

Per-hour proxy: HT $18.50/hr · NK $16.67/hr · Painting + Sun $28.33/hr. A 50–70% per-hour premium over our existing workshops — defensible because of the small-group cap (half of HT/NK), the named artist, the take-home, and the live astronomer.

The line for Matt. "Matt — we are pricing this excursion based on what it actually is: a 3-hour small-group product capped at 24 guests, with a named local artist, all watercolor materials, a take-home painting per guest, a 60-minute interactive dome experience with a live local astronomer, and private van transport. The biggest cost in the stack is the transport — the operators charge us in Mexican pesos, and with the exchange rate at roughly 16 MXN per USD (it was over 20 a year ago), every transport invoice translates to more dollars on our side. We have priced it tight to the experience — nothing speculative."

So · the quote

Open: $85 USD per pax · VAT included · min 8 · max 24. Floor $80, only traded for a real commitment (slot guarantee, multi-quarter, or a Celebrity Shorex landing-page feature). Operating windows ~8:30 AM and ~14:30.
