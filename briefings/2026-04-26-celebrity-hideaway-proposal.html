A Shore Excursion Proposal

Cozumel Hideaway.
Two new excursions.

A premium small beach club and a fully private cabana experience, both at Cabana Beach — a secluded venue inside Chankanaab Park, the gateway to the Cozumel Reefs National Marine Park. Submitted for consideration by cruise line Destination Experiences teams.

The proposal · in brief

Two products. One private venue.

Visit Cozumel proposes adding two excursions to your Cozumel shelf, both operating from Cabana Beach — a small, intimate beach club at the south end of Chankanaab Park, not currently marketed to any other cruise line. Both products include round-trip private transport, Chankanaab Park entry, an on-site chef preparing a plated lunch, and an open bar.

Product A · Beach Club

A premium small-group beach experience with chef-prepared lunch, open bar, and direct reef snorkeling. $149 net per guest, minimum 8 guests to operate.

Product B · Private Cabana

A fully private cabana — one party only, no sharing — with the same inclusions. $895 net per cabana, flat, for 1–6 guests. Runs every day, no cancellations.

Both products · one venue

Beach Club and Cabana share the same transport when both are booked, allowing your team to offer two distinct guest experiences at one shore-time, with one operational footprint.

Product A

Cozumel Hideaway: Beach Club & Caribbean Feast

An exclusive small beach club at Chankanaab Park, with chef-prepared lunch, open bar, and direct reef snorkeling.
$149
USD net per guest

Leave behind the worries of everyday life for a tropical getaway and enjoy the amenities of Cozumel's most exclusive small beach club, including direct reef snorkeling, a chef-prepared lunch, and an open bar.

Board your air-conditioned van and enjoy a 15-minute scenic drive to Cabana Beach, a small private retreat tucked into the south end of Chankanaab Park. This is a secluded venue that operates only when a small group is booked — never crowded, never shared with another tour.

Soak in the tropical sun by the Caribbean's pristine waters, sip a refreshing cocktail from the bartender, and drift to the soothing sounds of the waves. Snorkel directly off the legendary Chankanaab Reef, part of the Cozumel Reefs National Marine Park, just steps from your lounger — no boat, no waiting.

Prepare to be pampered — the venue comes with a gracious bilingual host, attentive waiters, and an on-site chef who prepares a delicious plated lunch: two house dishes and a dessert, paired with an open bar of soft drinks, ice-cold beer, and domestic spirits.

Feeling adventurous? Explore Chankanaab's full grounds — the natural lagoon, gardens, and trails are all yours, since park entry is included. A relaxing massage from on-site experts is available at an additional cost. After unforgettable memories, return to your awaiting ship feeling fully refreshed.

Chankanaab Park · included access

Steps from the cabanas. The whole park, included.

Access to Chankanaab Park is included with your bracelet in your individual guest fee. Steps from the cabanas and accessible from the beach, visit the park to enjoy the different attractions and activities they have to offer.

The park houses the natural lagoon, marine gardens, jungle trails, and the legendary Chankanaab Reef — part of the protected Cozumel Reefs National Marine Park system.
