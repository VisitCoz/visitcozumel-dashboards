What this means

finding 1 · we are entering an empty niche

No existing TUI tour in Cozumel is under 2.75 hours. Our 2.5-hour Plaza + Dome tour would be the shortest tour in the catalog. There is no direct duration competitor — every other tour requires 3+ hours of a guest's day.

finding 2 · 4–5 hours is the peak — we sit below it

The largest cluster (6 tours) is 4–5 hours. The median TUI guest in Cozumel commits 5 hours to a single excursion. We are at half that — explicitly targeting the guest who wants to be back on the ship for lunch, or to combine our tour with another short stop.

finding 3 · €45 pricing is defensible

At €45 / 2.5 h = €18 per hour. Atlantis Submarine (2.75 h) likely retails €100+ = €36+/hour. Beach tours at 4–6 hours likely retail €50–70 = €10–15/hour. Our €/hour is in the middle of the catalog — premium tech (the dome + telescope) justifies it, but the lower total ticket makes it an easy add for guests who already booked something longer.

finding 4 · the "second tour" play

A 2.5-hour tour can be paired with another short tour on the same day. A guest doing Atlantis Submarine in the morning (2.75 h) could buy our tour in the afternoon (2.5 h) and still be back on the ship by 16:00. We are not competing with the 4–8 hour tours — we are the second tour someone adds on. Make this point to intercruises.
